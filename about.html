What is this?

This tool visualises the dynamics of the entry level job market for medium-skilled occupations

For each medium skilled occupation, users can explore:

  • the level of demand
  • salaries offered by employers
  • the level of competition job seekers face
  • locations with the strongest job prospects

Who is this for?

This tool is for learning providers, employers and job seekers of medium-skilled jobs.

We focus here on medium-skill roles to help all stakeholders in this vulnerable class of jobs align credentials to current employer demand.
